About

Gach Rud Ina Áit

Gach rud ina áit — Irish for "everything in its place" — is a personal recipe book built with Flask and PostgreSQL.

Store, organise, and discover your favourite recipes. Add new dishes, filter by category, and save your favourites — all backed by a live database.
